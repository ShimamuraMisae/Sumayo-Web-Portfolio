About the Project

The system was developed to support the Information and Communications Technology Office (ICTO) of Biñan in efficiently managing their inventory assets. It includes multiple modules designed to streamline operations such as asset tracking, report generation, user management, and audit logging. The platform features a user-friendly interface and a robust backend architecture to ensure smooth performance, data integrity, and accessibility for ICTO personnel.

My practicum experience at ICTO Biñan has been a valuable stepping stone in my journey as an IT professional. The challenges I encountered pushed me to grow, and the lessons I learned will continue to guide me as I pursue future opportunities in the field of web development and beyond.
